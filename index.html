Welcome to the website of Le Cercle Francophone, UCLA’s one-and-only French Club! Founded by Professor Laurence Denié-Higney in 2000, Le Cercle Francophone has a history of over 20 years and has had hundreds and thousands of members. On this website, you will be able to find different resources that help you learn French, various opportunities for students, and activities organized by the UCLA French Department.

Due to COVID, our weekly club meetings that were held in Room 236 of Royce Hall before are currently all held on Zoom. Please join our Facebook group and email list and follow us on Instagram to stay up-to-date on our events. We look forward to seeing you during Winter Quarter!

*As this is a website newly designed and built, please forgive us if you experience any inconvenience.
