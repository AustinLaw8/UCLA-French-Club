Pi Delta Phi


Pi Delta Phi is the honorary national society of the French. It was founded in 1906 at the University of California, Berkeley and is the oldest French honorary society in the United States. The Gamma Chapter of UCLA was founded in 1926.
Interested? contact Laurence Denié-Higney: laurence@humnet.ucla.edu

Summer In France


France Summer Program: Spend the month of July in Paris while improving your French. You can finish your 'language requirement' by taking Curriculum I (French 2 and 3), or continue your studies with Curriculum II (French 4 and 5). For those who want to do a minor or major in French, you can take the curriculum III (French 100 and 107). Interested? Contact Laurence Denié-Higney: laurence@humnet.ucla.edu

L’Indécis au Précis


L’Indécis au Précis is an annual academic journal that publishes articles on French and Francophone cultural subjects in the humanities and social sciences. The journal is supported by the department. You can participate by becoming an editor or you can submit an article.
If you are interested, contact the journal directly: lindecisauprecis@gmail.com
